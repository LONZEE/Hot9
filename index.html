About Hot 9 Golf

From early mornings on the course to late-night swings in the backyard, golf has always been where I feel most at home.

Over the years, I’ve learned that the heart of the game isn’t just in perfect shots — it’s in the community, the challenge, and the joy of improving one swing at a time.

That passion is what inspired me to open Hot 9 Golf. I wanted to create a space where golfers of all levels can play, practice, and have fun — no matter the weather or season.

Whether you’re looking to sharpen your skills, experience world-class courses, or simply enjoy a great time with friends, our goal is to bring the best parts of the game indoors.
