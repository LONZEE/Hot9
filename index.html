Simulators & Facility

Tour-level feedback without leaving town.

Our launch monitors and simulators track ball speed, spin, face angle, path, and more. You’ll see exactly why that last shot was a baby draw or a wipey slice.

FAQ

Questions before you book your first hot nine?

A few quick answers. Update these with your real policies.

  • Do I need to bring my own clubs? +
    You’re welcome to bring your own clubs — that’s always our recommendation. We also keep a small selection of house sets you can borrow if you’re just getting started.
  • How long does a HOT 9 round take? +
    For two players, a 9-hole round typically takes 45–60 minutes depending on pace. Larger groups or first-time simulator players may want to book a 90-minute block.
  • Are beginners welcome? +
    Absolutely. We love working with new golfers. We can set you up with beginner-friendly settings, aim assists, and optional coaching sessions.
  • Can I host a private event or company outing? +
    Yes. We can reserve multiple bays, help design formats, and build a simple scoreboard. Reach out with your date, head count, and goals — we’ll take it from there.
