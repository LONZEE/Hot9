About Hot 9 Golf

From early mornings on the course to late-night practice sessions, golf has always been at the center of what we do.

Over the years, we’ve learned that the heart of the game isn’t just perfect shots — it’s the challenge, the community, and the pursuit of getting better one swing at a time.

That passion is what inspired Hot 9 Golf. We built a space where golfers of all skill levels can play, practice, and compete year-round — regardless of weather or season.

Whether you’re dialing in your swing, playing iconic courses from around the world, or spending time with friends, our goal is to deliver a premium indoor golf experience.

Let’s play a Hot 9.
